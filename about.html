Our Story

Founded in 2024, Inference Agents started with a simple observation: UK small businesses were getting left behind while big companies embraced AI.

We saw entrepreneurs struggling with the same problems - expensive staff, missed leads, endless hiring cycles. Meanwhile, enterprise companies had armies of AI tools at their disposal.

So we built something different. Not a complex enterprise system. Not a team of consultants. Just proven AI agents that work, at a price small businesses can afford.

Today, we've helped 50+ UK businesses save over £2.1 million combined. And we're just getting started.
