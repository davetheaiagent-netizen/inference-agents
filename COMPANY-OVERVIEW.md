## Full Company Overview

### Who We Are

Inference Agents is a UK-based AI automation agency that helps small businesses deploy autonomous AI agents to handle sales, marketing, customer service, and operations.

We're on a mission to make enterprise-level automation accessible to every UK business - not just big corporations with massive budgets.

### The Problem We Solve

UK small businesses are stuck:
- Hiring is expensive (£3,000-5,000/month per employee)
- Staff burn out, take holidays, get sick
- Missed leads = lost revenue
- Scaling means more hiring = more problems

### Our Solution

We deploy AI agents that:
- Work 24/7/365 (no sick days, holidays, or burnout)
- Cost 97% less than hiring staff
- Set up in days, not months
- Handle sales, marketing, support, HR, operations & more

### What Makes Us Different

✓ **38 Ready-to-Deploy AI Agents** - We don't build from scratch - we have proven agents for every business function

✓ **UK-Based Support** - Real people, real help, when you need it

✓ **Fast Setup** - Most agents live within 7 days

✓ **Proven Results** - £2.1M+ saved for clients
